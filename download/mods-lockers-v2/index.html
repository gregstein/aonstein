Mods Lockers V2

A small tool that stops Age of Empires 2 Definitive Edition from disabling your mods or adding anniversary/achievement mods. Comes very handy when a new patch is released. Resulting into, all the mods you are subscribed to get disabled or lost .
Mods Locker is an open source tool created by myself and available at my GitHub repo: Mods Locker For AoE2 DE
